  • About Code Master

    Welcome to Code Master Hackathon,
    exclusively for currently enrolled students of ESOFT Metro Campus - Matara. Join us for the first annual competition, Code Master 1.0, in 2023, organized to challenge and showcase your coding skills and creativity. With Code Combat, Mind Maze, Blitz Code, and Bullet Brain challenges, you'll be pushed to your limits, with industry experts on hand to judge and provide mentoring. This event is not just about winning but also about learning,
    so don't miss out on this amazing opportunity to develop new skills.
